## SHKeeper Features

SHKeeper is a powerful self‑hosted cryptocurrency payment processor with a range of features designed to give you full control, flexibility, and security when accepting crypto payments.:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

### Key Features

1. **Multi‑Currency Support** – Accept payments in many different cryptocurrencies and switch between them easily.:contentReference{index=1}  
2. **Direct Crypto Payments** – Payments go straight from customer wallets to your system without intermediaries.:contentReference{index=2}  
3. **Customizable Rates, Fees & Commissions** – Set your own exchange rates, fees, and commissions.:contentReference{index=3}  
4. **Overpayment Credit** – If a user overpays, SHKeeper automatically credits the difference to your balance.:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  
5. **Partial Payments** – Users can make payments in installments.:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  
6. **Auto‑Payments to Cold Wallets** – Automatically move funds to cold storage based on schedule or balance.:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}  
7. **No Transaction Fees to Third Parties** – SHKeeper does not charge transaction fees to intermediaries.:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}  
8. **No Third‑Party Involvement** – No external processors mean greater privacy and censorship resistance.:contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}  
9. **Increased Privacy & Security** – Full control over keys and reduced reliance on external services.:contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}  
10. **No KYC Required** – No need for identity verification procedures.:contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}  
11. **Non‑Custodial** – You retain complete ownership of your private keys and funds.:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}  
12. **Independent, Open Source Software** – Fully open‑source and community‑driven software.:contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}  
13. **SegWit Support** – Enhanced transaction flexibility and efficiency.:contentReference2{index=13}  
14. **Easily Embeddable Payment Buttons** – Simple integration of payment buttons into any web resource.:contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}  
15. **Full Internal Wallet with Hardware Wallet Integration** – Use SHKeeper as a full crypto wallet with advanced features.:contentReference{index=15}

These features make SHKeeper a flexible and powerful choice for businesses looking to integrate direct cryptocurrency payments while keeping full control over funds and infrastructure.:contentReference{index=16}
