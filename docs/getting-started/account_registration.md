# Account Registration

Creating an account is the first step in setting up SHKeeper and gives you administrative control over your instance. :contentReference{index=0}

---

## 🔹 Step‑by‑Step Registration

1. **Open SHKeeper URL**  
   - Navigate to your SHKeeper dashboard URL in a browser.  
   - You will be automatically redirected to the admin password setup page. :contentReference{index=1}

2. **Set Your Admin Password**  
   - Enter a strong password.  
   - Confirm the password.  
   - Click **“Set password”**. :contentReference{index=2}

3. **Access the Dashboard**  
   - After setting the password, you will be able to log in to the SHKeeper dashboard using your new admin credentials. :contentReference{index=3}

---

## ✉️ Contact & Support

If you need help during registration or want custom integration, you can send a request directly from the registration page:

- Subject  
- Email  
- Message  
- Submit via **Send** button. :contentReference{index=4}

---

### 📌 Notes

- Registration is fast and automatically creates your admin account. :contentReference{index=5}  
- The admin password you create is required to log in and manage your SHKeeper instance. :contentReference{index=6}
